Design Specs

The specs for the engineering challenge are posted here. Our take on the app contains all requirements for the challenge, plus a few extra ones we thought might add a nice touch:

  • A user profile service for users to generate and sign in to their accounts
  • Historical tracking for click generation
  • Custom input fields on drip pages for background colors, banner images, informational column layout, and special text/image fields
  • Pages for users to track their most successful campaigns based on number of clicks
  • Statistics for several fields that track, for instance, which banner images or backgrounds generate the most clicks
  • A functioning Django backend?
